Sean's Hometown

Sean is from Orlando, FL, best known as the home of Disney World. But there's more to it than that:

Yes, Sean's been to Disney a lot--everyone in Orlando knows someone who can get them in for free.

But theme parks are overrated. For Sean, the real attraction is less than an hour away, Florida's beaches.

Here are some of Sean's favorite beaches, ranked:

  1. Anywhere in St John, USVI
  2. New Smryna Beach, FL
  3. Sanibel Island, FL
  4. Melbourne Beach, FL
  5. Horseneck Beach, MA

As a result of being from Florida, Sean is quite used to its subtropical climate and is thus quite cold in Cambridge.
